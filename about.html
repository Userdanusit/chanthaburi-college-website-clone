About Our College

Discover our rich history, innovative programs, and commitment to excellence in technical education

Our History & Heritage

Established in 1985, Chanthaburi Technical College has been at the forefront of technical education in Eastern Thailand for nearly four decades. What began as a small vocational training center has evolved into a comprehensive technical institution serving thousands of students.

Our college was founded with the vision of bridging the gap between academic learning and practical skills needed in the rapidly evolving technological landscape. Over the years, we have consistently adapted our curriculum and facilities to meet the changing demands of industry and society.

Today, we stand as a testament to the power of technical education in transforming lives and communities, with over 15,000 graduates who have gone on to successful careers in various technical fields.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality technical education that empowers students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to excel in their chosen careers and contribute meaningfully to society's technological advancement.

Our Vision

To be the leading technical college in Thailand, recognized for excellence in education, innovation in teaching methods, and the success of our graduates in the global marketplace.
