Achievement November 25, 2024

Academic Excellence Awards 2024: Our Students Shine at National Competition

Chanthaburi Technical College students have achieved remarkable success at the National Technical Skills Competition 2024, securing multiple gold and silver medals across various categories. This outstanding performance demonstrates the quality of education and training provided by our institution.

The competition, held in Bangkok from November 20-24, 2024, brought together the best technical students from across Thailand. Our students competed in categories including mechanical engineering, information technology, electrical systems, and business innovation.

Key Achievements:

  • 3 Gold medals in Mechanical Engineering Technology
  • 2 Silver medals in Information Technology
  • 1 Gold medal in Electrical Engineering
  • Special recognition for Innovation in Business Solutions
